WordSTAT

WordSTAT is a simple web application for the analysis of words in texts. It reads direct input (in different encodings) directly from the internet and it produces word and keyword frequency lists, monolingual and bilingual concordances from these inputs.

No conversion needed, just input text to analyze your corpus...
